# AI-Powered Legal Awareness & Case Risk Advisor

## Problem Statement
Many students and common citizens lack basic legal awareness and do not understand the risks involved in legal cases. This leads to poor decisions, fear, and lack of access to justice.

## Solution
This project proposes an AI-powered Legal Awareness and Case Risk Advisor that:
- Explains legal concepts in simple language
- Provides basic case risk analysis
- Helps users understand possible outcomes
- Improves access to legal awareness for communities
